Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide our company specific information to the user. These would be items like why we do what we do, what we do and how we do it. This site would provide our contact methods and a way to sign up for an excurion.

Audience

This site will provide information to people looking for a new and wonderful experience or a repeat of a previous thrill that they couldn't get over. The individuals coming to this site will be from all walks of life, nearly any age, as long as health issues are not a problem. They will be able to gather the majority of the information they need to chhose us as their host and have the additional options to ask further questions if needed. They will also be able to schedule expeditions directly online.

Typography

Heading Font: Open Sans, sans-serif

I found several websites that listed these two fonts as top choices because they work well together. They are both crisp and extremely legible. They also work well if they are swapped around.

Paragraph Font: Roboto, sans-serif

Same comment as above.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.
