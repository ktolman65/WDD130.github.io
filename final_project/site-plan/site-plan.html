Overview

Purpose

Have you ever gone to a movie without checking its reviews or asking friends what they thought about it? Not many people do. Well, the same could be said regarding hiking in Utah. With so many hiking trails Utah has to offer, where does one start to explore? My website will provide that friendly review and detailed information that the average visitor or even local resident will be happy to use when making their hiking choices. This website will provide simple directions and details regarding the actual hike. Every hike on my site will be one I have taken personally. The site will also include reviews from myself and others that have ventured these trails. With this information, anyone can enjoy the magnificent beauty of the wonder we call Utah.

Audience

Content The individuals coming to this site will be from all over the world with a plan to hike right here in our back yard. They will be from all walks of life, nearly any age looking for various levels of hiking options. They will be able to gather the majority of the information they will need to choose the best hike for their situation. They will also be able to look through reviews left by other hikers to help make their choice.
